PWA for Enhancing Vocabulary

Summary

Articulate is an innovative educational web application designed to enhance vocabulary acquisition and retention. It leverages AI to provide comprehensive definitions, example sentences, and personalized feedback on user's application of new words or phrases in sentences. Users can keep track of their progress as they build their vocabulary and review their personalized vocabulary lists.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React, Ionic, DaisyUI, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend/Database: Python Flask, Firebase Firestore, Firebase Auth
  • AI API: Gemini Pro API
