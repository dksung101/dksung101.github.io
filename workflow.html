WorkFlow

Personal Task Management App

Summary:

WorkFlow is a versatile and user-friendly application designed to help you organize your thoughts, tasks, and ideas conveniently.
  1. With a focus on simplicity and functionality, it offers a seamless note-taking experience, allowing you to effortlessly create, edit, and delete notes.
  2. The app includes a built-in weather section to keep you informed about current weather conditions, enabling you to plan your day more effectively.
  3. Additionally, the Notes App features a Pomodoro Timer with 25-minute work sessions and 5-minute breaks to boost productivity and manage time effectively.Users can track their progress with four X marks that gradually turn into check marks, symbolizing the completion of an optimal two-hour session.
  4. Furthermore, the app provides theme flexibility with both light and dark modes, allowing you to customize the app to suit your preferences and enhance readability in any lighting condition.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Flutter, Dart, MaterialUI, Lottie
  • Backend/Database: Isar Database
  • Libraries/APIs: OpenWeatherMap API, Timer_Count_Down Library
